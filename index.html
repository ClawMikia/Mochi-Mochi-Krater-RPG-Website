About

Mochi-Mochi Krater is a browser-based monster battle game inspired by classics like Pokemon Deluge and Pokemon Crater. No traditional game elements here—just you, your team, and the arena. Gather 200 uniquely named monsters, build your perfect team, and fight your way through CPU opponents or challenge online players.

Features:
• 200 comedy-named monsters with stats from 250-300
• 1v1, 2v2, 3v3 battle formats
• Player vs CPU & Player vs Player modes
• Type-based effectiveness system
• Supabase-powered online matchmaking
