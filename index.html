About

Mochi-Mochi Krater is a browser-based monster battle game inspired by classics like Pokemon Deluge and Pokemon Crater. No traditional game elements here—just you, your team, and the arena. Gather 238 uniquely named monsters, build your perfect team, and fight your way through CPU opponents.

Features:
• 238 comedy-named monsters with stats from 250-300
• CPU battle mode
• Type-based effectiveness system
• Supabase-powered matchmaking
