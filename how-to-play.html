HOW TO PLAY

Master the arena
1. Build Your Team

Head over to the Team Builder to assemble your squad. You can select up to 3 monsters. Each monster has different stats (HP, ATK, DEF) and types that determine their effectiveness.

2. Enter the Battle

Once your team is ready, go to the Battle Arena. Choose your match format (1v1, 2v2, or 3v3) and practice against the CPU with varying difficulty levels.

3. Battle Mechanics

• Turns: Battles are turn-based. You and your opponent take turns attacking or switching.
• Attacking: Select a move to deal damage. Damage depends on ATK, DEF, and type effectiveness.
• Victory: The battle ends when all of an opponent's monsters have fainted.

4. Type Effectiveness

Some types are Super Effective (2x damage), while some are Not Very Effective (0.5x damage).

Examples:
• Fire is strong against Ice.
• Water is strong against Fire.
• Electric is strong against Water.
